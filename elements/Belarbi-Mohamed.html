Belarbi Mohamed(European champion)

Belarbi Mohamed, a world-class pugilist who disappeared at the age of 85 in total anonymity and ingratitude. European champion in his category from the age of 14 and an insanely talented boxer, Belarbi Mohamed was also a trainer behind the emergence of several generations of renowned pugilists. Engaged in the fight for national liberation from the first hour, the deceased led a fierce fight against the French occupiers alongside a fabulous career as a boxer steeped in qualities.Known for his uppercuts which often send his opponents to the mat and his fatal right, the deceased spent many months hospitalized in several establishments until his disappearance on tiptoe, in the most total ingratitude. Loved and respected by everyone in Tiaret as everywhere in the country, he was a convinced patriot and madly in love with his country, until his last breath. Accompanied by a large crowd from the city of Tiaret, he was buried in the cemetery of Oued Sellam, in the wilaya of Relizane, where he is from.
