Hamdani Adda

Born April 26, 1926 in Tiaret, chahid Hamdani Adda is the only boy in a family of four girls. Cereal trader in the early fifties, he joined the FLN networks of Tiaret before joining the maquis in 1957. He was quickly appointed head of the autonomous sector of Tiaret before directing all the operations carried out by the fidayine. He then sets up a women's network before being promoted to head of region one (Zone VII - Wilaya V). Hamdani Adda was arrested on December 4, 1959 in a cave in Oued Lilli with six elements of the ALN, placed under his orders. He suffered the worst tortures and psychological actions on the part of the French army who tried to extract a confession from him but in vain, given the unparalleled courage of the chahid. Appearing before the court of Tiaret, then that of Oran, he was sentenced to death for the fourth time in his heroic combat journey. Incarcerated, if Othmane takes the head of a steering committee of the prison of Oran and engages in the demands to improve the prison conditions of the Algerian prisoners. In April 1961, Hamdani Adda and three of his fellow prisoners were kidnapped by an OAS commando on the orders of four putschist generals. They were all four burned alive on a sad day on January 12, 1962. Today, no one knows where the body of Si Othmane lies, a bit like saying from under his grave to his executioners without knowledge or conscience that the heroes never die...
