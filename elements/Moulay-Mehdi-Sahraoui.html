Moulay Mehdi Sahraoui

Boxing champion in several former competitions, former president of the Tiaret wilaya football league, Moulay Mehdi Sahraoui, this former guerrilla from Wilaya V, now 89 years old, has just been honored in Algiers by the Algerian Boxing Federation.

Moulay Mehdi Sahraoui ended last Friday, April 1, 2022, his 89th birthday. He becomes the dean of Algerian boxing. Boxing champion in several former competitions, former president of the Tiaret football wilaya league, he has just been honored by the Algerian boxing federation in Algiers.

In professional, he had recorded 32 fights including that of champion of Paris in light weight against Tessier, 1 defeat and 3 draws. His career has been marked by more ups than downs. He had, among others, crossed the gloves with Rezoug, Godih, Daidi, Adjal, Germano Cavalieri and was consecrated fourth lightweight in Europe.
